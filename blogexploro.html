Computational Thinking & Coding: Essential 21st Century Skills

We live in an age where technology plays a major part in our lives. We have 'n' number of apps that can manage our travel, food, fitness and everything in-between. Although Children know how to use Software and Smart products, they seldom understand how they work. Now educators all around the world realize that this gap needs to be filled as forecasts indicates that computer-related jobs will rise by 30 per cent by 2025, with a strong demand for software developers.


The goal here is not about equipping our future generations to be software engineers but to teach children the basis of computational thinking at an early age.

Google for education define computational thinking as " a problem solving process that includes a number of characteristics, such as logically ordering and analyzing data and creating solutions using a series of ordered steps (or algorithms), and dispositions, such as the ability to confidently deal with complexity and open-ended problems"

In other words computational thinking can teach children to solve complex problems by breaking them in to manageable parts. Getting such an orientation at an early age can help children understand how technology works because computational thinking concepts are at the core of technology.


Exploro Trinity and Exploro Blocky are systems with both hardware and software designed to teach the basic of computational thinking and coding to children in a classroom setting. With the included curriculum, its easy for schools to integrate computational thinking course in to their existing teaching methodology.

Exploro Blocky

Exploro Blocky is a modular Robot designed to teach children the basic concepts of computational thinking and coding. Blocky has blocks which can be snapped together to perform different activities. Blocky can be programmed by the simple 'Drag & Drop' C-Blocks software.

Exploro Trinity

Exploro Trinity is a versatile coding platform which can help students design & build their own robots and automation systems. Exploro Trinity is powered by the extensive C-Blocks software.

C-Blocks Software

Coding is fun and interactive with C-Blocks software. The simple and versatile 'Drag & Drop' software is simple enough to master and sophisticated enough to make amazing projects and robots.

Benefits of computational thinking stretches beyond that of software. This approach of problem solving finds applications in almost all fields such as physics, space science, micro-biology, music, mechanical engineering, communication technologies etc.


By teaching Children the concepts of coding and computational thinking we can prepare them to take on the challenges of future. It doesn't matter if they never become software engineers. Computational thinking & coding are skills for life.
