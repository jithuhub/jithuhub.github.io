How is this computational thinking?


Let us consider a possible scenario while playing with Draw-Me. The problem statement for a young girl could be something like "Help Draw-Me buy some clothes for princess in the castle". For solving this problem girls will need to route Draw-Me to the boutique and then to the castle. This problem solving thought process could be her first training towards computational thinking. The scribbling she will do to route Draw-me could be a her very first algorithm. Once a child's mind picks up this problem solving approach, she can soon scribble more complex algorithms.
